About RateMyRations

RateMyRations lets the UIowa community see dining hall menus and rate items. It aggregates ratings at the food, station, meal, and dining hall levels.

How ratings work

  • Your rating: saved in this browser. You can change it anytime.
  • Community rating: average of all ratings. Shows count and distribution.

Data source

Menus are fetched from the UIowa Nutrislice API and cached for performance.

Privacy

No personal accounts. A random browser ID is used to enforce one rating per item per browser.
